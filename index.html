Moving Forward— We believe that students deserve a real voice at Georgetown: an advocate for issues that actually affect us. GUSA spends too much time worrying about its own bureaucratic concerns rather than bringing students together to fully address issues like accessibility, affordability, and student health and wellness. Made up of an often-insular Executive and the same group of Senators each year, GUSA has failed to empower every student to have an active role in shaping the Georgetown community. We want to combine our energy and institutional knowledge with perspectives from every corner of campus in order to truly empower all Georgetown students. That’s why we’re running for student body president and vice-president–because GUSA should be the student association. We plan to elevate every voice as an important tool in these conversations. It’s time to move GUSA forward–to truly make it our student association–so we can all move forward together.
